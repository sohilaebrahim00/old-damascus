# OLD DAMASCUS — CLIENT ASSET DROP INSTRUCTIONS

Drop your files into the correct subfolder below.
**You do not need to rename any files.** The system will process them automatically.

Supported formats: `PNG  JPG  JPEG  WEBP  AVIF  SVG  PDF  DOCX  XLSX`

---

## 📁 Folder Guide

---

### logo/
**What to drop here:**
- The official Old Damascus logo file(s)
- Any version of the logo you have (transparent background preferred)
- Multiple formats are fine (e.g. PNG + SVG)
- Color version AND white/reversed version if available

**Do NOT drop:**
- Unofficial or old versions
- Clipart or generic icons
- Edited/modified versions

**Example files:**
```
old-damascus-logo.png
logo-white.svg
logo-transparent.png
OD_Brand_Logo.ai.png
```

---

### menu-photos/
**What to drop here:**
- Individual food photos from your kitchen
- Individual drink photos
- Any photo that shows a specific dish or item

**Naming tips (optional — not required):**
- If a photo shows "Shish Tawook", you can name it `shish-tawook.jpg`
- If the name is unclear, just drop it as-is — we will match it manually

**Do NOT drop:**
- Stock photography downloaded from the internet
- AI-generated food images
- Blurry or very low-resolution photos (under 400px)

**Supported formats:** `JPG  JPEG  PNG  WEBP  AVIF`

---

### menu-source/
**What to drop here:**
- Your printed or digital menu (PDF, photo, scan)
- Screenshots of your Clover menu
- DoorDash or Uber Eats menu screenshots
- Price list documents (Word, Excel, PDF)
- Any document containing: item names, prices, descriptions, categories

**Examples:**
```
OldDamascusMenu_2025.pdf
menu-prices.xlsx
clover-export.csv
menu-screenshot.jpg
```

**Why this matters:**
This is the source of truth for all menu data. The more complete the file, the more accurate the website menu will be.

---

### restaurant/
**What to drop here:**
- Exterior photos of the restaurant building
- Interior dining room photos
- Kitchen photos
- Staff photos (if approved for use)
- Location / parking photos
- Any photo that represents the physical restaurant

---

### catering/
**What to drop here:**
- Photos of catering setups
- Event photos featuring Old Damascus food
- Catering trays and platters
- Any photo taken at a catering event

---

## Important Rules

1. **Do not delete or move any file** after dropping it — the system reads the originals in place.
2. Drop files in the folder that best matches the content.
3. If a file could belong in two folders, put it in both — duplicates are fine.
4. You may drop files any time. The website will not be modified until you confirm with **ASSETS READY**.

---

## When You Are Done

Reply to the developer with exactly:

```
ASSETS READY
```

The system will then scan all files, match them to the correct menu items and pages, and integrate them into the website automatically.
